FACULTY COORDINATOR

Dr. Pallab Biswas (HOD ME Dept.)

Dr. Pallab Biswas is the faculty co-ordinator of our Society, Euphony. He is the HOD of the Mechanical Engineering Department of our College. He is a part of the College choir. He is an amazing Singer himself and encourages us to perform better each time making us a better team.

PARTICIPATION

Team Euphony, the musical society of the college represents the college in various Inter-College Competition. A large number of teams from various colleges/universities participates in such mega event to showcase their talent.
The basic events that our society takes part in are:
  • Battle of Bands (Band Wars)
  • Indian Solo Singing
  • Classical Solo Singing
  • Duet Singing
  • Group Singing
  • Acapella
  • Instrumental Solo
The members of the team had new experiences to cherish upon as high level of their competence was put to test that led them to explore new realms of creativity and also bring laurels to the college in the process.
As a result of continuous hard work that the team puts into such events, the team always brings home Victory in the form of awards and titles. Winning trophies is not our only goal. Its the amount of new things that we learn in the process that counts. You can see it well from the achievement section.
